# Fanmerch

FanMerch is an ecommerce app that provides influencers with a unique platform to sell their merchandise to their fans. The app is built ReactJS, Django, MySQL, and the Razorpay payment gateway, providing a seamless and secure ecommerce experience. With features such as product listings, shopping cart, order tracking, and user account management, FanMerch provides a comprehensive solution for influencers to sell their products to their fans. The app is designed to be scalable and customizable, allowing it to be tailored to meet the unique needs of each influencer and their fan base.
